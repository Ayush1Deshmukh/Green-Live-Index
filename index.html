About Green Living Index

Green Living Index provides a comprehensive evaluation of sustainable practices and environmental impact. This framework is built on the latest sustainability metrics and is designed to help you understand and improve your environmental footprint. You are free to use this index for personal or commercial purposes.

A Step Towards a Greener Future

Redistribution of the Green Living Index data or materials is not allowed without explicit permission.

Commit to Sustainability

Please share information about the Green Living Index with your network. Thank you for contributing to a greener planet.
